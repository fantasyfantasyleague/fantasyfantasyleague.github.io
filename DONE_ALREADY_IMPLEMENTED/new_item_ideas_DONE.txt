scope --> accessory gives passive to increase ranged weapon range by 1. ONLY WORKS ON BOWS AND CROSSBOWS AND MAGIC RANGED WEAPONS.

Grappling hook --> offhand. Can pull any unit that is 2-5 squares away to you.
